Private Party

Our parties are structured and scheduled outside of open gym and class times so your kids get to take over the gym! You have a gym instructor leading your child and friends through an obstacle course, games, assistance on the equipment, and of course, free time!

Schedule includes:

  • 30 minutes in the party room
  • 60 minutes in the gym with an instructor
  • Tons of FUN!

Hours available:

  • Friday – after 5pm
  • Saturday – after 3pm
  • Sunday – all day

Open Gym Party

Your party takes place during our regularly scheduled open gym time. The children have access to all of the equipment to play and have fun. This includes 30 minutes in the party room prior to open gym.

Schedule includes:

  • 30 minutes in the party room
  • 60 minutes in the gym during open gym
  • Tons of FUN!

Cost:

$150 (for 15 kids including birthday child)

Additional children – $8 each

Hours available:

  • Monday, Wednesday, Friday – 12-1 pm
  • Saturday – 2-3:30 pm
